Get to know our team of professionals!

Bronson Priest

A catch-as-catch-can wrestler, who will twist you in a knot. Bronson Priest is known as the go-to guy if you want to have that true, old-school feel of the wrestling days long since passed.

Hunter Shawn Angel

Hunter Shawn Angel is a world-renowned Mixed Martial Artist with a devastating style. He comes to Knuckles Championship Wrestling to train talent to have the most convincing strikes as possible.

Michael 'The Animal' Goldbraith

Michael 'The Animal' Goldbraith is unpredictable, yet calculating in his maneuvers. Students will learn the art of selling and in-ring psychology.

Marty Alex

Marty Alex here is a cocky and brash wrestler, yet cowardly when it comes down to matching up with his opponent in the ring. Marty excels in promo work and carrying an air of "heel" charisma - something no doubt will be a valuable teaching asset.

Nicolas Johnson

Nicolas Johnson will have you cheering for his underdog ways as he fights for the gold. Nicolas a "babyface" magnetism that attracts the crowds and summons their cheers. If you're likeable, Nicolas is the go-to instructor.

Conan Daniels

A wrestler with a tragic backstory, Conan Daniels strives to help his community and clean his past as a former felon. This, of course, never happened but Conan is a creative mind, an asset that trainees will discover to be important in crafting their persona and character.
