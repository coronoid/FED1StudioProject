If you are interested in wrestling for us, we have classes! Whether a coach, a manager, or a wrestler, here are classes for you!

Class Subjects

Promo Skills

Hook crowds to your story by "cutting a promo", speaking hype into it via a microphone.

Selling

Make spectators believe what is happening in the ring during matches.

Techniques

Use your skillset to tell a story and deliver your brand and style of fighting.

Character

Develop your persona, giving your character a backstory, personality, and goals.

Safety

Looking out for the safety of everyone involved in the ring.

Kayfabe

The importance of not breaking character by living by the storyline.
