Make your dreams come true!

Be a part of the best niche in wrestling!

The place for true, gritty professional wrestling!

Are you done with horrible theatrics and want something that feels truly raw? Do you want something so explosive, yet so believably brutal that you wince, but cannot look away - instead, staring enamored by the performance in front of you?

Should this interest you, then Knuckles Championship Wrestling is the promotion for you! Whether you be a fan or a talent in the business, you have a home here.

KCW prides itself on the old school wrestling presentation, with the comforts of modern wrestling styles. With managers harken back to Bobby Heenan and Paul Bearer, charismatic speakers are welcome. If that isn't your thing, then perhaps commentators will suit you, as you call the action as it plays out in the ring. As for our referees, you too can be an Earl Hebner or Charles Robinson! And finally, for those who toned up their athletic and combative abilities, we are proud to announce that we have a diverse blend of styles and feel strongly about inclusivity. Tap in, or tap out!

For interested talent, take a look at our team, our classes, and should this strike your fancy, feel free to contact us on our Contacts page!
